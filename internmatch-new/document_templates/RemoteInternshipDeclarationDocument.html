Remote Internship Declaration (Host Company)

In line with the requirements of InternMatch and our education partners, please confirm you have read and understood the requirements of the InternMatch Remote Internship declaration.

I acknowledge that

An appropriate induction and onboarding session will be organised for each intern at the beginning of each internship.
Interns will be provided with the mechanisms for regular communication with their supervisors using contemporary communication software.
Eg. Phone, Slack, Email, Skype, Zoom, Teams.
We will schedule daily meetings with the intern and at least one member of staff.
We will complete an end-of-week summary of work completed and formal feedback.
If we identify that the intern's work environment is not suitable, we will make the placement coordinator aware immediately.
To the best of our ability, we will provide opportunities for the intern to expand their professional network.
Eg: Arranging meetings for them with other staff members
We will support students to complete their daily logbook entries.
We are required to provide periodic feedback on each intern as requested through InternMatch.

I acknowledge that I have read and understood the InternMatch remote internship declaration and will be responsible for providing instructions to all our staff who elect to supervise Interns remotely.
