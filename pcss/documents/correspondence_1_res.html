Batemans Bay Bridge replacement project - Offer of a building condition inspection

Roads and Maritime is committed to minimising impacts to properties during construction of the Batemans Bay Bridge replacement project. While we do not anticipate any impacts to your property, we have arranged for local company Surety Property to carry out building condition inspections on residential properties near the project area.

Why is a building condition inspection required?

The purpose of the inspection is to provide the property owner with a record of the structural condition of your property before major work starts. Surety Property have qualified and independent inspectors that will attend your property and take photos and develop a report on the external & internal condition of all buildings. A copy of the building condition inspection report will be provided to you and a copy will be kept by Roads and Maritime. There is no cost associated with this inspection.

The inspections are scheduled to start in October 2018 and the process should take around one hour.

How do I schedule an inspection at my property?

Please call Surety Property on 1300 767 741 or email bbaybridge@suretyproperty.com.au to organise a time to have your property assessed.
