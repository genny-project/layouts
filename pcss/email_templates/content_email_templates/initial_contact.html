Property pre-condition survey

It is standard practice on major projects to offer property pre-condition surveys to adjacent properties. This provides an important reference document for both the property owner and the contractor in the unlikely event of any accidental property damage. At the completion of works, a property post-condition survey will be offered to properties that have had a pre-condition survey undertaken. We may also need to conduct periodic property inspections during construction, however we will contact you in advance to arrange this.

Your property has been identified as being located within an area where construction activities are taking place. We are therefore offering you the opportunity to have a complimentary property pre-condition survey undertaken.

The purpose of the survey is to record the current condition of your property before major construction activity begins. The survey will include an inspection and photographs of the inside and outside of any buildings, sheds, garages, fences and paths.

An independent contractor will be engaged to undertake the property pre-condition survey. All companies engaged have extensive experience in property condition assessments and the preparation of condition survey reports.

The survey is expected to take approximately (Indicate approx. time to complete eg: 1 hour or 30 Minutes) to complete and you or a nominated representative will need to be present during the inspection. A copy of the pre-condition survey report containing photographs of your property will be provided to you at no cost. You will also have the opportunity to verify the survey report. We will confidentially maintain copies of the report throughout the construction period.

Please note a separate survey of your building will be offered through your building manager or body corporate. This will include a survey of the building and potentially the installation of settlement monitoring devices.
